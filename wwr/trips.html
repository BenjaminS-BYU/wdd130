Trips

Ripple Run

Enjoy a fun, beginner-friendly float down a calm stretch of river with gentle rapids and beautiful scenery. Perfect for families or first-timers, this trip offers a relaxing ride with just enough splash to keep things exciting.

Canyon Dash

Take on a mid-level rafting adventure through a narrow canyon filled with quick turns, small drops, and crystal-clear pools. Ideal for those seeking a short yet thrilling ride, with a perfect mix of excitement and breathtaking views.

Rapid Rush Express

Dive straight into the action with this fast-paced run packed with back-to-back Class III rapids. Designed for adrenaline seekers who want maximum thrills in minimum time — you’ll finish soaked, smiling, and ready for more.
